Ekal Skiksha

Ekal Shiksha is an Ed-tech company which provides one on one individual online classes. It is an initiative which aims to revolutionize the standard teaching and learning methods. We believe that each student has his or her personal learning speed, method and curriculum which is very important for a teacher to understand and personalize their teaching methods according to the students not vice versa. This platform is especially built for those who have immense potential but are never focused on in group classes, the growth of such students is our moto.
